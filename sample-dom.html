Staff Notes
Coaches Video:
*Note - for the partner version of today's workout we decided to adjust the format after Mike filmed this video. The version written is the correct version NOT what Mike says in the video





Strength:
Strength Notes:

Today's focus is building pressing strength. The weight on the bench press should be a tough load from the start. Athletes should only add weight if they're able to hit 5 reps. On the bench press, remind athletes to keep three points of contact-- shoulders and butt on the bench, and fee on the ground.
For the HS hold, athletes will accumulate as much time in 1 minute as possible. If athletes cannot hold on the wall, they can put their knees on a box and stack their shoulders as much as possible or can go to a plank hold.

"Mix and Match":
Metcon Notes:
Target- The goal for today's intervals is repeatable efforts. Athletes should try to match their AMRAP scores of the two sets of Toes to Bar and their AMRAP scores of Push-ups. They should scale the jump rope to something that can be done smooth and ideally in 1-2 sets (a few trips is okay). The push-ups should be scaled to keep high quality reps with a tight midline. Break the toes to bar movement into repeatable set sizes. Athletes should have at least 1 minute to work on the AMRAP movement or they should scale the buy-in.

Feel- High intensity intervals

Flow- Athletes will perform the double under and push-up buy in, then AMRAP toes to bar until three minutes. They will rest 1 minute, then perform the double under and toes to bar buy-in, then AMRAP push-ups until three minutes. They will rest one minute and repeat this all one more time.

Scaling-
For further scaling on Toes to Bar and Double Unders see Beyond Scaling Document: https://www.trainingthinktank.com/s/ttb-tier2-scaling-4jfn.pdf, https://www.trainingthinktank.com/s/DU-tier2-scaling-pejk.pdf



Extra Credit:
Extra Credit Notes:

This is a back off week of total volume.
Athletes will run at their 45 min pace (RPE 4). This should be slower than the 5k pace they held last cycle! Athletes should try to match their distance each set.
If athletes are unable to run, this same workout can be performed on a rower!
